Lady Dufferin | Cowasjee School Of Midwifery


Beach Luxury Hotel on 25th Feb 2015.



An event was organized by Pakistan American Culture Center (PACC) with the collaboration of Karachi Youth Initiative (KYI) at Beach Luxury Hotel on 25th Feb 2015.



Various organizations were invited to place their stalls to raise awareness on different academic and professional areas for the development of youth.



School of Midwifery Lady Dufferin Hospital Karachi also participated. This was the first time that the midwifery students exposed their self in front of the youth of Karachi and brief them about the profession of midwifery and its scope.



LDH, CSOM stall was highly appreciated by the participants and organizers and more than 500 youth were benefitted by this activity.
